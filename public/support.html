Getting Started

Welcome to Synq — a premium music player built for listeners who care about sound quality. Here's everything you need to get started.

Download Synq from the App Store or Google Play, create a free account, and import your music library. Synq supports all major audio formats including FLAC, AAC, MP3, and Hi-Res LPCM. Your library syncs seamlessly across all your devices.

Once your library is set up, explore personalized playlists, smart queues, and our precision EQ — all designed around your listening habits.

Frequently Asked Questions

Synq supports a wide range of audio formats: FLAC, ALAC, WAV, AIFF (lossless), MP3, AAC, OGG Vorbis, Opus (lossy), and Hi-Res formats up to 32-bit / 384kHz. MQA and DSD playback is available on supported hardware.

On iOS: use the Files app to place your audio files in the Synq folder, or use the Share Sheet from any supported app. On Android: Synq automatically scans your device storage for music files. You can also import from cloud storage (Google Drive, Dropbox) via Settings → Cloud Sources.
Synq features a 10-band parametric EQ with presets for popular headphone models. The AI-powered Auto EQ automatically analyzes your headphone's frequency response and applies the optimal correction curve. You can also create and save your own presets.
Sign in with your Synq account on all your devices. Your playlists, queue history, EQ presets, and listening stats will sync automatically. Music files themselves can be synced via iCloud (iOS/macOS), Google Drive, or a custom NAS connection.
Subscriptions are managed through the App Store (iOS) or Google Play (Android). Go to your device's account settings → Subscriptions → Synq to modify or cancel. Your access continues until the end of the current billing period. For refund requests, please contact support.synq@gmail.com.
Audio stuttering is usually caused by insufficient buffer size or background app limitations. Go to Settings → Playback → Buffer Size and increase it to "Large" or "Max." On iOS, ensure Synq has background app refresh enabled. If problems persist, please contact us with your device model and OS version.
You can delete your account from the app: Settings → Account → Delete Account. This permanently removes all your data, playlists, and preferences from our servers. Local files on your device are not affected. Alternatively, email us at support.synq@gmail.com and we'll process the deletion within 30 days.
