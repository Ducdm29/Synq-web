Compact uppercase pill for statuses and labels (e.g. "MỚI", "LOSSLESS", "ĐANG PHÁT").

```jsx
<Badge tone="accent" dot>Đang phát</Badge>
<Badge tone="neutral">Hi-Res</Badge>
<Badge tone="accent" solid>Mới</Badge>
```

`tone`: `accent | neutral | danger | warning`. `solid` for filled, `dot` for a leading status dot.
