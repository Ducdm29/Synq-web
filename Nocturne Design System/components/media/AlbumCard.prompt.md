Cover card for albums, playlists, and artists — used in grids and horizontal rails.

```jsx
<AlbumCard cover={art} title="Hoàng Hôn" subtitle="Vũ." onPlay={play} />
<AlbumCard cover={pic} title="Sơn Tùng M-TP" circle />
```

A mint play button fades in on hover. `circle` makes a round artist cover (centers the labels).
